Sars Cov2

Concept

SarsCov2 is a satirical video project that targets high-ranking politicians, humorously pitching the creation of a virus to drive society to self-destruction through human stupidity. With dark humor and sharp commentary, the video exposes the absurdities and flaws in our behavior and decisions, offering a provocative critique of today's social and political landscape.
