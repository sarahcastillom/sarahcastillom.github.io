El día que llegaste

Concept

El Día que Llegaste is an illustration project that captures the essence of experiencing love at first sight. It aims to evoke the overwhelming emotions and sensations one feels when encountering the love of their life for the first time. With delicate and expressive visual language, the artwork portrays the magical moment of connection and recognition, where time seems to stand still and the world fades away. The illustration invites viewers to immerse themselves in the beauty and wonder of that transformative moment, reminding us of the power and intensity of true love.
